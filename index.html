about

In 2019, I left the hustle of San Francisco for my hometown in the mountains of the Sierra Nevada, trading a rocking, rolling, creaking houseboat for a drafty log cabin. When not working, I can often be found with skis strapped to my feet, or dripping and shivering near an alpine lake. My husband and two lovely, grubby children are usually nearby, probably sneezing on something.
