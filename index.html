about

In 2019, I left the hustle of San Francisco for my hometown in the mountains of the Sierra Nevada, trading a rocking, rolling, creaking houseboat for a drafty log cabin. When not working, I can often be found with skis strapped to my feet, or dripping and shivering near an alpine lake. There's generally a half-complete art or sewing project in my bag. And Dave the dog and my two lovely, grubby children are usually nearby, probably sneezing on something.
